# CLAUDE.md

**OpenClaro** — the customer-experience platform for local businesses, delivered as done-for-you AI modules. Any action that AI can do, AI should do. Humans lead, oversee and guide. Frame work in terms of the **measurable outcome** each module delivers. Pricing is custom per module and business — quoted after a free consultation, not a blanket public commission rate (see [ADR 0005](docs/adr/0005-custom-per-module-pricing-replaces-commission-only.md), which supersedes the old free-to-start/commission-only claim). Risk removal for the customer comes from the Triple Guarantee below, not from a fixed "pay only on results" formula. We are solving a problem you are facing and we want to do it NOW. No BS, no financial risk. Leave any time.

**Core competency:** OpenClaro makes every customer-facing touchpoint — website, Google Business, Yelp, social, calls, texts, reviews — look and feel like one consistent, premium brand, powered by AI the customer should never have to notice. The product ships as six modules — **Brand, Presence, Concierge, Growth, Reputation, Insights** — "lego pieces" a business can pick up and embed to take autonomous action with minimal-to-no human involvement. Concierge (voice/text/chat agents) is just one of six modules today, not the center of the pitch — don't let phone/call or "AI" framing dominate messaging or the homepage; lead with the customer-experience/revenue outcome, not the technology. Each module should read as interchangeable and equally weighted; the platform and its modularity is the story, not any single channel. Picking up more modules is what makes a business more efficient, streamlined, predictable, and on-brand.

**OpenClaro's Tripple Gurrantee:** 
- Leave any time
- Money back gurranteed on a monthly basis! 
- Website and all the presence will be returned back to when we started. 

## Our mission: 
- Enable businesses to be open about what AI can do to various functions of business
- Add clarity to your business by offloading any action that AI can handle.
- Add human touch where need be
- Your business will work like a well established $100 M organization: 
  - predictable
  - reliable
  - systematic
  - on-brand 
  - at all times
- Everything will be logged and will be ready to be optimized via our analytics.  

**Thesis:** Businesses run on manual, ad-hoc, inconsistent customer experience that AI can now standardize and run autonomously — a missed call, a cold lead, an empty chair, a stale Google listing, a slow review request. Each module plugs one specific, measurable gap and is sold on ROI ("recover N jobs/month and the fee pays for itself"). The compounding effect of picking up more modules — not any one channel — is the core competency.


## Modules

- **Brand** — standardize look, voice, and messaging across website, Google Business, Yelp, and social so every touchpoint reads as the same company.
- **Presence** — keep listings, local SEO, and photos accurate and ranking, continuously.
- **Concierge** — voice, text, and chat agents that answer, book, explain, and follow up like a premium front desk.
- **Growth** — reactivation, referrals, reminders, and campaigns that turn contacts you already have into booked revenue.
- **Reputation** — review requests, responses, and negative-review interception that compound local ranking.
- **Insights** — the monthly report: what was recovered, what was missed, and what it's worth.
